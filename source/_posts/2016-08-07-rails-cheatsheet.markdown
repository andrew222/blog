## rails new app_name

  > 参数：
  >>  * `--skip-bundle` \# 跳过bundle
  >>
  >>  * `--database=postgresql/mysql` \# 指定数据库

## Generate命令
  > scaffold
  >> `rails g scaffold Client name:string`

  > controller
  >> `rails g controller Client`

  > model
  >> `rails g model Client name:string`
